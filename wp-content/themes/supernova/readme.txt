~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
Supernova Theme
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Supernova is a free theme with lots of intutive features, it has been designed specially for bloggers however it was kept in mind that the theme should also be usefull for other purposes as well.
